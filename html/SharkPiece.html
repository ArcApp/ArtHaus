Parents, amirite?

About this Image:

This true .gif masterpiece tells the most wonderful and tear-filled life of a shark in haiku form.

  1. I'm a baby shark
    My mama feeds me plenty
    and I love to swim
  2. My parents are sharks
    Biting off my head all day
    I'm losing my mind
  3. It's fun in college
    A big shark in a small pond
    My parents are proud
  4. Making moolah now
    I'm having a lot of fun
    swimming 'round the world
  5. Have shark fam'ly now
    Settled in a real nice spot
    Living the good life
  6. Take fam'ly to see
    Shark mama and shark papa
    But no one is home
  7. Hear from old neighbor,
    "Orca came and ate them up"
    Hands me note from mom
  8. CHOMP CHOMP CHOMP CHOMP CHOMP
    CHOMP CHOMP CHOMP CHOMP CHOMP CHOMP CHOMP
    Parents, amirite?
